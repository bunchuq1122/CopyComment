# <cb>You can copy comments!</c>
With this <cy>mod</c>, you can easily copy any <cb>comment</c>.

- <cg>How to use:</c>  
Simply press the <cr>Copy</c> button in the <cy>top right corner</c> of a <cj>comment</c>.

<cj>Select the copy option in the mode settings</c>
- <cr>copy without the username</c>
- <cr>Don't show the popup</c>
